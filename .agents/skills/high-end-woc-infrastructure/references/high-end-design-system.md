# High-End Design System (Agent Reference)

## Goal
Create websites that look premium and client-specific in first 3 seconds.

## Required Signals

1. **Distinct concept**
- Hero must communicate a specific business character, not generic category copy.
- Surprise Element must be visible on page 1.

2. **Typography hierarchy**
- Headline register: bold and specific.
- Supporting text: short, readable, contrast-safe.
- Avoid repetitive generic labels (e.g., "Our Services", "Why Choose Us").

3. **Section rhythm**
- Alternate visual weight (dark/light/surface).
- Avoid monotone section stacking.
- Keep CTA progression intentional (discovery → trust → conversion).

4. **Image direction**
- Use imagery with mood and business context.
- Avoid generic stock feeling.

## Rubric (0-5 each)
- Brand specificity
- Visual hierarchy
- Typography quality
- Rhythm/spacing
- Conversion clarity
- Motion restraint

Target: `>= 22/30` with all hard gates passing.
