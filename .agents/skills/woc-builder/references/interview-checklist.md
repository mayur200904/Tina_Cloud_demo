# Client Interview Checklist

A quick reference for builders conducting the coffee chat intake. Ask in a natural, conversational order — not as a formal survey.

---

## Core Questions (Must Answer)

1. **Business name** — exactly as it should appear on the site
2. **What do you do?** — one sentence description
3. **Who are your customers?** — their industry, size, persona
4. **Top 3–6 services/products** — with short descriptions if possible
5. **What city/region do you primarily serve?**
6. **Do you have a logo?** — if yes, get the file; if no, note we'll use text

---

## Differentiation Questions

7. **How long have you been in business?**
8. **What's your biggest competitive advantage?** (price, speed, expertise, relationships)
9. **Any notable clients or partners we can mention?**
10. **Any certifications, awards, or industry memberships?**
11. **Do you have testimonials or Google reviews we can use?**

---

## Content Questions

12. **What feeling should visitors get from the site?** (professional, warm, bold, minimal)
13. **Do you have any existing photos or do you need stock images?**
14. **Is there a primary CTA?** (call, book, request a quote, shop)
15. **What's the contact email/phone that should appear on the site?**
16. **Physical address?** (needed if brick-and-mortar)

---

## Technical / Handoff Questions

17. **Do you own a domain?** If yes, what is it? If no, walk them through Namecheap/GoDaddy.
18. **Who will update the site?** (client self-manages via TinaCMS admin)
19. **Do you need a contact form or just a phone number?** (formspree required if form)

---

## Quick Niche-to-Profile Decision

| Their Answer to "What do you do?" | Profile to Use |
|---|---|
| Lawyer, accountant, consultant, financial advisor | Corporate Professional |
| Doctor, dentist, gym, salon, cafe, restaurant, retailer | Friendly SMB |
| Factory, engineering, construction, logistics, manufacturing | Industrial |
| Software, app, tech product, startup, agency | Modern Startup |
